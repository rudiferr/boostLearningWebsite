OUR TEAM

All our tutors are experts in at least one subject area and trained in the Boost Learning model and curriculum. Our tutors are state-certified teachers, college students, and college graduates with at least 4 years of tutoring in an area such as math, reading, writing, SAT/ACT, etc. We currently offer virtual tutoring nationally and in-person tutoring in Northern Jersey. We have tutors serving the following areas: Bergen County, Passaic County, Hoboken, and Union County. We are always looking for highly qualified tutors and teachers to join our growing team!
